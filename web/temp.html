Longing for the Moon Afar

Zhang Jiuling A bright moon rises from the sea, Lighting up the world at this very moment. Lovers complain about the distant night, Missing each other from dusk till dawn. Extinguishing the candle, I regret the faint light, Putting on clothes, I feel the cold dew. Unable to offer a handful, I turn over, pitying myself.
